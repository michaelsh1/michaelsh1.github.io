Intervalue

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Intervalue ("the App") is an interval timer and calisthenics training application for iOS, developed by Michael Sharon ("I", "me", or "developer"). This privacy policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information I collect

Intervalue does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you create within the App — including workout history, custom intervals, and exercise logs — is stored locally on your device.

Data storage

Your data stays on your device. Specifically:

  • Workout sessions and history are saved locally using on-device storage.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or crash reporting SDKs are included.

Third-party services

Intervalue does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from users under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this is satisfied by default.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future update, I will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
