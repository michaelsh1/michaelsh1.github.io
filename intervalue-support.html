Frequently asked questions

How do I create a custom interval?

Tap the + button on the main screen to create a new interval. You can set the work duration, rest duration, number of rounds, and give it a name.

Can I use the app without sound?

Yes. The app works in silent mode. Visual cues on screen indicate phase transitions even when audio is off.

How do I delete a workout?

Swipe left on any workout in the list to reveal the delete button.

The app crashed — what should I do?

Please email me with your device model and iOS version, and a short description of what you were doing when it crashed. This helps me fix it quickly.
