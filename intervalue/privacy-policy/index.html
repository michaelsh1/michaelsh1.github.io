InterValue Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. InterValue is designed to work entirely on your device — your workout data never leaves your phone.

What We Collect

Local Data Only

All workouts, settings, and preferences you create in InterValue are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal workout data.

AI-Generated Workouts

InterValue includes an optional AI feature that generates workout plans from your text descriptions. When you use this feature, your prompt text is sent to a third-party AI provider (OpenRouter) to generate a response.

No personal identifiers (name, email, location) are attached to these requests. Please avoid including personal health information in your prompts.

OpenRouter's privacy policy governs how they handle requests: openrouter.ai/privacy

What We Don't Collect

No Accounts, No Tracking

  • We do not require or collect account information (no email, no sign-in).
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or health data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

App Store & Apple

Apple & TestFlight

When you download the app through the App Store or TestFlight, Apple may collect certain diagnostic and usage information according to your device privacy settings. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Data & Device

Deleting Your Data

Because all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by removing workouts within the app or by uninstalling InterValue from your device. iOS will remove all associated data when the app is deleted.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out: support@intervalue.app
