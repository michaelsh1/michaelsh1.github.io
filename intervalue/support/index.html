Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new workout?

Tap the + button on the main screen. Give your workout a name, then add intervals using the Add Interval button. Each interval has a name, duration, and color you can customize. Tap Save when you're done.

Can I reorder intervals within a workout?

Yes. While editing a workout, press and hold the drag handle (the three lines icon) on any interval row and drag it to the desired position.

How do I set the number of rounds?

In the workout editor, you'll find a Rounds setting. Tap it to set how many times the full interval sequence repeats. The total workout duration updates automatically as you adjust rounds.

Does the timer run in the background?

Yes. Once a workout is running, you can lock your screen or switch apps and the timer will continue. Audio cues and notifications will fire at each interval transition.

What does the AI feature do?

The AI assistant can generate a complete workout for you based on a natural language description — just describe your goal (e.g. "20-minute HIIT with 3 exercises") and it will create the intervals automatically. Usage is limited to a small number of requests per day.

How do I change the app theme or colors?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and choose between System, Light, or Dark mode. Individual interval colors can be changed inside the workout editor by tapping the color swatch next to each interval.

My data disappeared after reinstalling. Can I recover it?

InterValue stores all workouts locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, local data is removed by iOS. Make sure iCloud Backup is enabled on your device so your data is included in device backups. Cloud sync between devices is not currently supported.
